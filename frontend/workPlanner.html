WORKOUT PLANNER

Push-Pull-Legs (PPL):

Divides workouts into three categories: push exercises (chest, shoulders, triceps), pull exercises (back, biceps), and leg exercises (quads, hamstrings, calves).

Typically follows a 3-day or 6-day weekly cycle.

Push Day :

Exercise Name Sets x Reps
Bench Press 4x8
Shoulder Press 3x10
Tricep Dips 3x12
Bicep Curls 3x15

Pull Day :

Exercise Name Sets x Reps
Pull-Ups 4x8
Deadlifts 3x10
Bent Over Rows 3x12
Face Pulls 3x15

Leg Day :

Exercise Name Sets x Reps
Squats 4x8
Lunges 3x10
Leg Press 3x12
Calf Raises 3x15

Divides workouts into upper body and lower body sessions.

Often follows a 4-day weekly cycle, with two upper body and two lower body workouts per week.
